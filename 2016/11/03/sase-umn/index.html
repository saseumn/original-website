SASE UMN

Posted on November 3, 2016November 3, 2016 by hwunrow

The SASE UMN chapter at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities is one of the region’s strongest and most established chapters, creating excellent leadership and volunteering opportunities for their membership. This upcoming year, SASE UMN plans to focus on the SASE Jr. initiative by providing high school students early exposure to STEM and providing a mentorship program between collegiate and high school students.

Location: Minneapolis, MN
Formed: 2011
Members: 60

About

"SASE UMN is a student organization at the University of Minnesota that is dedicated to the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment so that they can achieve their full career potential. In addition to professional development, SASE also encourages members to contribute to the enhancement of the communities in which they live. SASE’s mission is to: Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world. Celebrate diversity on campuses and in the workplace. Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities."
